Projects

  • Fujairah Whale and Dolphin Project

    Marine mammal observer and leader of the first dedicated cetaceans project in the Emirate of Fujairah, focusing on the identification and long-term monitoring of whales, dolphins, and other marine mammals.

    Success: Identified 14 cetacean species, including 3 dolphin species newly recorded in UAE waters.

  • Genetic Analysis of Common Bottlenose Dolphin

    Led the first genetic diversity study of common bottlenose dolphins in the UAE, establishing collaboration with leading experts and gaining valuable insights into the genetic makeup of these species.

  • Marine Biodiversity Assessment

    Comprehensive evaluation of the diversity and health of key marine species in Fujairah marine waters, aimed at informing effective conservation strategies and management practices.

  • Artificial Reef Project

    Project leader in building artificial reefs using oyster shells and palm leaves for coral rehabilitation and biodiversity conservation. The project began with an Environmental Impact Assessment, followed by reef design, construction, and implementation.

    Success: Deployed 14 artificial reefs using various materials and secured funding of $15,000 from Atlantis.

  • Water Quality Monitoring

    Established a continuous monitoring program to assess the impact of human activities on reef habitats, focusing on heavy metals, microbial analysis, pH, and salinity.
